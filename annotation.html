Tagging Instructions (Click to expand)

Goal

We will show you two reviews from the same hotel. Highlight word sequences in these two reviews talking about the same aspect of the hotel.

Aspects refer to properties/attributes of products or services. Examples of aspects could be food, service, pool, room, dog allowance, cleanliness, taxi, etc.

Constraints

  • You can select at most 6 different aspects.
  • Colors are here to help you distinguish different aspects. You can select a new aspect on the right panel.
  • For each of these, you must highlight a word sequence in both reviews talking about the same aspect (using the left click on a word and moving the cursor).
    • For instance, it is possible that the first review consists of word sequences of only one aspect (i.e. one color) and the second of several aspects (i.e. multiple colors) or vice-versa. In this case, only higlight the overlapping aspect.
  • Select the shortest span of texts, for example:
    • "This is the most beautiful hotel I have ever stayed in ."
    • "The last day we were there we went down to breakfast, and the food wasn't all that great."

Invalid submissions

You will not be able to submit an answer if:

  • You higlight an aspect in only one review and not the other.
  • You do not highlight any aspect.
  • You select less than three words.
